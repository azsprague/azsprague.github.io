Design, Build, Fly! at VT - SEC Representative & Outreach Coordinator

October 2019 - Present

DBF was the first club I joined at Virginia Tech; each year, we create an unmanned, electric powered, radio-controlled aircraft to be applied to "real world" missions. It's a pretty substantial task which involves months of work and dozens of workers, so members are divided into sub-teams where they focus on specific parts of the process. As a Freshman, I joined the Manufacturing team - we were responsible for creating the physical plane from the CAD models.

Alongside Manufacturing, I shadowed the club's Student Engineer's Council Representative and Outreach Coordinator (Jack Barnes) to learn more about how the club gets funding and promotes itself. Fast-Forward to the 2020-21 school year, and here I am in that exact position! My exact duties entail:

  • Representing the Club Among All of the Engineering Clubs
  • Allocating Club Funding from the SEC
  • Organizing and Filing Receipts
  • Communicating with the Project Manager
  • Planning Outreach Events to Promote the Club
