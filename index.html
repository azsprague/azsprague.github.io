Who Am I?

I'm an aspiring Software Developer, UX Designer, and Entrepreneur from the Northern Virginia area. I'm a second-year Computer Science student at Virginia Tech with a minor in Human-Computer Interaction and a focus in Artificial Intelligence. I currently hold two club leadership positions, plan to serve as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for the Computer Science Department in the upcoming Spring semester, and have a Summer 2021 internship planned with AnaVation, LLC.

Software design has been a big passion of mine for years. To me, creating a system, algorithm, or website yourself is almost its own form of art; the satisfaction that comes from seeing something that you made being used and enjoyed by others is second to none.
